2.3 Model covariates

According to the DAG produced by C. Philippat and M. Rolland (figs/DAG/dagitty-model_2020-11-27.jpg) the final model covariates are:

  • Ethnicity
  • Exposure to other contaminant (smoking)
  • Maternal depression
  • Parental education
  • Parity
  • Maternal age
  • Child age (outcome predictor)
  • Time of experiment (outcome predictor)
